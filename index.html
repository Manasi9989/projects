  3. Diabetes Model

    This project revolves around building a model to diagnostically predict whether or not a patient has diabetes, based on certain diagnostic measurements. The dataset is originally from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases. Several constraints were placed on the selection of these instances from a larger database. In particular, all patients here are females at least 21 years old of Pima Indian heritage. The final model was calculated out to be 80.5% accurate.
